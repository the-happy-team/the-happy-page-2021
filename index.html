About
The Happy App is an artistic project, an experience and a creative tool, interpolating machine learning with human emotions.

In constant update, the app uses facial emotion recognition technology to evaluate your mood while in front of the screen. How absurd is that quantification?

Looking for ways to deal with codes on the edge of our capitalist surveillance system, aware of the instability and controversial aspects of this technology, this is a playful experience that respects data policies and user’s privacy.

If you prefer, you can call it a SELF THERAPY WEB APP 2.0, and it is free.

Share your thoughts with us: info@thehappyapp.art

Created by Mari Nagem in partnership with Thiago Hersan.
Mari Nagem is a multidisciplinary artist exploring the artificiality of landscapes, interested in anthropological and environmental transformations through technology. She is currently reviewing the notions of delight, raising existential questions with a sense of irreverence while trying to bring sensibility to the relationship with and along the digital.
